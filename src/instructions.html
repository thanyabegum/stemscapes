Stemscapes

A scene-based stem player.


How to Play

Toggle these instructions with the I key.

  • Use the arrow keys or WASD to move from quadrant to quadrant, triggering the different stems of a song
  • Look around with the mouse
  • Click on Sustain or press = to sustain or unsustain the current stem
  • Choose a song from the dropdown to change the music
